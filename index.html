About the Church of Christ – Puerto Princesa City

The Puerto Princesa City church of Christ is not a part of any denomination (I Cor. 1:10-13). We are simply a local body of Christians (I Cor. 1:2) who meet together to worship the Lord (Acts 20:7) and who do God's work together (I Tim.5:16).

We have no clergy (Matt. 23:8-12); the church is composed of bishops (pastors, elders), deacons, and saints (members) (Phil.1:1). We follow no human creeds (Rev. 22:18-19) but only Christ and His Word (Eph. 1:22-23). Our worship is in spirit and in truth (John 4:23-24), not according to human tradition.

“Let us speak where the Bible speaks and be silent where the Bible is silent. Let us call Bible things by Bible names and do Bible things in Bible ways.”

Members are called to live holy lives (I Pet. 1:15), and our one hope is a home in Heaven (Eph. 4:4).
